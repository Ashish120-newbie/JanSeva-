# JanSeva - AI-Powered Unified Citizen Services & Smart Governance Platform

## Overview

JanSeva is an AI-powered unified citizen services platform designed to simplify access to government services through a single intelligent interface. It enables efficient grievance management, transparent complaint tracking, and AI-assisted decision-making for both citizens and government authorities.

---

## Problem Statement

Citizens often struggle with fragmented government portals, repetitive paperwork, delayed grievance resolution, and lack of transparency. Government departments also face challenges in complaint routing, prioritization, and monitoring.

JanSeva addresses these challenges using Artificial Intelligence to improve accessibility, efficiency, and governance.
